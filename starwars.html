A long time ago in a university not so far away...

Episode II
SOEN 344

It is a dark time for software engineering. Students are unable to reserve study rooms with equipment, and the old system is no longer enough.

Tasked with the maintenance and improvement of the Concordia University Room Reserver, a group of students known as Team 14 has proposed a refactor and redesign of epic proportions.

Armed with a talented team of developers, Team 14 set out to vanquish the evil forces of High Coupling and Low Cohesion in the farthest reaches of the source code...
